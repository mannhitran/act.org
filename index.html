About ACT

ACT is a Midwest regional prevention and youth development organization dedicated to helping young people make informed, healthy decisions.

Mission Statement

To serve as the statewide prevention catalyst, empowering individuals and fostering partnerships to promote safe, healthy, and drug-free communities.

Who We Serve?

  • Youth and young adults
  • Schools and educators
  • Community coalitions
  • Nonprofit organizations
  • Public health agencies
