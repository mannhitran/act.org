Our Mission

To serve as the statewide prevention catalyst, empowering individuals and fostering partnerships to promote safe, healthy, and drug-free communities.

Our Vision

Healthy individuals… Healthy communities…

About ACT

ACT Missouri is a prevention-focused nonprofit dedicated to empowering youth and communities to create safer, healthier, and drug-free environments.

👥 Our Team

Dedicated professionals and passionate volunteers committed to making a difference through evidence-based prevention programs and community engagement.

🤝

📚 Our History

For years, ACT has been at the forefront of prevention efforts in Missouri, building a legacy of positive community impact and sustainable change.

📖

🎯 Our Approach

We use evidence-based strategies and collaborative partnerships to create comprehensive prevention initiatives that address the root causes of substance abuse.

🎯

Our Programs

ACT offers comprehensive, evidence-based prevention programs designed to empower youth and strengthen communities across Missouri.

🎓 Youth Prevention

Targeted programs for young people focusing on substance abuse prevention, healthy decision-making, and building resilience through interactive education and mentorship.

🎓

🤝 Community Initiatives

Collaborative efforts with local organizations, schools, and families to create drug-free environments and strengthen community bonds through partnership.

🤝

📚 Education & Training

Professional development workshops and training sessions for educators, parents, and community leaders on prevention strategies and evidence-based practices.

📚

🌟 Leadership Development

Programs designed to cultivate emerging leaders who can drive prevention initiatives and create sustainable change in their communities.

🌟
